Features That Empower Your Mental Wellness Journey

At Mentara, we’ve thoughtfully designed our platform to support every step of your mental health journey. Whether you're a client seeking support, a licensed therapist offering care, or an administrator ensuring smooth operations — we’ve built features tailored for your unique needs.

Built for Safety, Trust, and Growth
With a secure and user-friendly experience, Mentara is committed to making mental wellness more accessible, inclusive, and empowering for all.

Secure Sign Up & Login

Clients can create a personal account and log in securely using encrypted authentication

Personalized Therapist

clients complete a quick questionnaire to help match them with therapists that suit their mental health needs.

Manage Appointments

Clients can easily manage their appointments, availability from their dashboard.

Virtual Therapy Sessions

Attend therapy through secure video, audio, or live chat — designed for comfort, flexibility, and convenience.

Smart Journaling

Clients can privately journal their thoughts and valuable insights for therapy.

Mood Tracking

Track daily moods through a simple interface with visual trends

Therapist Activities

Clients receive personalized tasks assigned by their therapist

Therapist Feedback

Clients can revisit notes from previous sessions

Reschedule or Cancel Appointments

Clients have the flexibility to reschedule or cancel sessions

Access Resource Library

Therapists may share articles, videos, or handouts

Notifications

Clients receive automated reminders for upcoming sessions

Confidential & Safe Experience

All client data, sessions, and personal inputs are protected by end-to-end encryption
