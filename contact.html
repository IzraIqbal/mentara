Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the right therapist?

Once you create your account and answer a few questions, we’ll match you with therapists who fit your needs and preferences.

Are my sessions private and secure?

Yes. All sessions are encrypted and completely confidential to protect your privacy.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Absolutely. You can easily reschedule or cancel your sessions from your dashboard.

Do I need a subscription to use Mentara?

You can browse therapists for free. Subscription or session payment is required only when you book a session.
